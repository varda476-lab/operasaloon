OUR LEGACY

Raipur's Premier Destination For Luxury Care

For over four decades, Opera Prime Salon has stood as a beacon of sophistication and high fashion in Civil Lines, Raipur. Founded in 1984, our legacy is built on a foundation of professional mastery, certified artists, and an unwavering commitment to client satisfaction.

We utilize only the world's most elite beauty brands—ensuring your hair, skin, and makeup receive the highest standards of care. Whether you are stepping in for a signature haircut, a therapeutic hair spa, or Raipur's most trusted bridal makeover, we promise an experience that rejuvenates your senses and elevates your beauty.
